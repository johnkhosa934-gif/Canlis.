Our Story

A Legacy of
Extraordinary Dining

Founded in 1950 by Frank Canlis, our restaurant has been a beacon of Pacific Northwest fine dining for over seven decades. Perched on a bluff overlooking the shimmering waters of Lake Union, Canlis offers more than a meal — it offers a moment suspended in time.

Now in its third generation under Brian Canlis, the restaurant continues to honor its heritage while embracing the innovative spirit of Seattle. Every dish tells a story of local ingredients, culinary mastery, and unwavering commitment to excellence.
